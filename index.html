Our Story

About
T&R Auto

T&R Auto Repair & Service was founded in December 2018 with a simple mission: to give the people of North West London a garage they could actually trust. We're based in Willesden's Park Avenue — right at the heart of NW10 — and we've built our reputation one happy customer at a time.

We believe great car care shouldn't cost the earth, and that you deserve to understand exactly what's being done to your vehicle. From a quick MOT to a complex engine rebuild, we bring the same professionalism, honesty, and care to every job.
